Common Questions

Everything you need
to know

Have questions about StarWell? We've got answers.
Still curious? Contact us anytime.

Do I need to know my exact birth time? +
While exact birth time helps create the most precise birth chart, StarWell still works wonderfully without it. We'll use 12:00 PM as a default time, which still provides valuable cosmic insights. Check your birth certificate if you want maximum precision.
What makes StarWell different from Calm or Headspace? +
While apps like Calm and Headspace offer excellent meditation content, they provide the same experience to everyone. StarWell personalizes your breathwork based on your unique birth chart and real-time planetary movements. Every day, you receive recommendations aligned to YOUR cosmic blueprint—not a generic playlist.
Is the astrological data scientifically accurate? +
Absolutely. StarWell uses Swiss Ephemeris, the same astronomical calculation system used by NASA and professional astronomers worldwide. Our planetary position data is accurate to 1/60th of a degree. While astrology is an interpretive art, our astronomical calculations are grounded in hard science.
How long are the breathing sessions? +
Sessions range from 3 to 20 minutes, with most protocols lasting 5-8 minutes. You can filter by duration to fit breathwork into your schedule—whether you have 3 minutes before a meeting or 20 minutes for deep practice. Every protocol shows its length upfront.
Can I use StarWell without believing in astrology? +
Yes! Many of our users are astrology skeptics who came for the breathwork and stayed for the personalization. Even if you don't believe in astrological influence, the breathing protocols themselves are traditional practices that may support general wellbeing. The cosmic matching helps you discover the right technique for you.
What's included in the free version? +
The free version includes 1 foundational breathing protocol (Deep Relaxation), your complete birth chart, daily cosmic guidance cards, and basic session tracking. You get to experience how StarWell personalizes recommendations before upgrading to access all 25+ premium protocols.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Canceling is easy. Go to Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select StarWell → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your billing period, and you can resubscribe anytime. No hard feelings—we're here when you're ready to return.
Is my birth chart data private? +
Yes, completely. Your birth chart and personal data are encrypted end-to-end and never shared with third parties. We take privacy seriously—your cosmic blueprint stays between you and the stars (and our secure servers).
