App Improvement and Analytics

  • Optimize breathing protocol effectiveness

Communication and Support

  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send important app updates and security notifications

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All personal data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data stored using iOS Keychain Services
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

Privacy by Design

  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information for app functionality
  • Consent Management: Clear opt-in for analytics and marketing communications
  • User Control: Easy access to view, modify, or delete your personal data
  • Transparency: Clear disclosure of all data collection and usage practices

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all personal information we have collected
  • Modify Information: Update your birth data, preferences, and account settings
  • Delete Account: Request complete deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export Data: Receive a copy of your personal data in a portable format

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

Age Requirement

StarWell is intended for users 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 16. If you believe we have collected information from someone under 16, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.
