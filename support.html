Getting Started

How do I create my birth chart?

To create your birth chart:

  • Open StarWell and complete the onboarding
  • Enter your birth date, exact time, and location
  • The app will automatically calculate your personalized chart
  • Your chart is used to generate daily personalized guidance

Note: Birth time accuracy is important for precise calculations. Check your birth certificate if you're unsure.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

If you don't know your exact birth time, you can:

  • Check your birth certificate (most accurate)
  • Ask family members who may remember
  • Use 12:00 PM as an approximate time

While the app will still provide valuable insights, some calculations may be less precise without an exact birth time.

How do I get started with breathwork?

To begin your breathwork journey:

  • Complete your birth chart setup first
  • View your Daily Guidance cards on the home screen
  • Tap any card to see recommended breathing protocols
  • Choose a protocol and follow the visual and audio guidance
  • Start with beginner-friendly protocols if you're new

Birth Chart & Astrology

How accurate are the astrological calculations?

StarWell uses Swiss Ephemeris, the same NASA-grade astronomical data used by professional astrologers worldwide. Our calculations are:

  • Astronomically precise to the second
  • Updated with the latest planetary position data
  • Validated against professional astrology software

Accuracy depends on the precision of your birth time and location.

Can I edit my birth information?

Yes! To update your birth data:

  • Open the app and go to Settings
  • Tap "Birth Chart"
  • Tap "Edit Birth Information"
  • Update your date, time, or location
  • Your chart and guidance will automatically recalculate

Breathing & Meditation

How do breathing protocols work?

Each breathing protocol is:

  • Scientifically designed for specific outcomes
  • Matched to your astrological influences
  • Guided with visual mandalas and audio cues
  • Adaptive to your current energy and needs

Follow the breathing pattern shown on screen and listen to the audio guidance.

Are breathing exercises safe?

StarWell's breathing protocols are safe for most people when practiced as guided. However:

  • Start slowly if you're new to breathwork
  • Stop if you feel dizzy or uncomfortable
  • Consult a healthcare provider if you have respiratory conditions
  • Avoid intense protocols if pregnant without medical approval

Listen to your body and practice within your comfort level.

How long should I practice each day?

We recommend:

  • Beginners: 5-10 minutes daily
  • Intermediate: 10-20 minutes daily
  • Advanced: 20-30+ minutes daily

Consistency is more important than duration. Even 5 minutes daily can provide significant benefits.
